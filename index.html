Exodus of Time


Exodus of Time is a sci-fi manga series created by Drake - aka Cactus Guts. Set on the desert planet E- 77, it follows Gero and Rae as they uncover psychic conspiracies, ancient prophecies, and the legend of the Eyes - a force said to manipulate time itself. Fueled by chaos, survival, and warped timelines, each chapter dives deeper into the cosmic unknown.


Saharan Rockin


Saharan Rockin is the independent studio and creative label behind Exodus of Time. Founded by Drake, it's a storytelling platform that fuses manga, glitchcore aesthetics, satire, and immersive workdbuilding. From experimental comics to Rae's Hotline broadcasts and beyond, Saharan Rockin exists to push the boundaries of indie manga and animation - and invite fans into the madness.
